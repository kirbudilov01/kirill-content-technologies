This is IT Tools: an open-source toolbox for developers that you can run locally in a few minutes.
It has generators, converters, JSON tools, JWT, Base64, hashes, cron helpers, and dozens of tiny utilities with no sign-up wall.
The AtlasRepo idea is simple: we do not just bookmark repos, we turn them into working playbooks.
Find the tool, launch it, verify it, record the demo, make the short review, and push it into the content engine.
That is what an AI scout should do: discover useful software early and turn every find into action.
