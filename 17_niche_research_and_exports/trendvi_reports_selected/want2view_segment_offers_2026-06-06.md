# WANT2VIEW Segment Offers

Date: 2026-06-06

## 1. Small YouTube Agencies

### Core Pain

They sell strategy, scripting, SEO, thumbnails, channel audits, and content calendars. The weak point is that research before strategy is manual, repetitive, and hard to scale across clients.

### Vulnerability

If their strategist spends 4-8 hours manually checking competitors and videos before every client plan, margin disappears. If they skip that work, the strategy becomes opinion-led.

### Offer

> WANT2VIEW becomes your pre-production research layer for every client niche.

### What To Show

- Competitor channel map.
- Outlier videos.
- Topic clusters.
- Audience questions.
- Title/hook patterns.
- Thumbnail references.
- 3-5 next video ideas.

### First CTA

> Send me one client niche or channel. I will run a free mini scan and send back a short research brief.

### Paid Next Step

Start with one full research report or one active workspace for one client. If useful, expand to agency workspace.

## 2. Personal Brand / Founder Content Agencies

### Core Pain

Their clients are smart but often do not know which topics the market actually wants. Agencies spend time extracting ideas, but those ideas still need demand validation.

### Vulnerability

Expert content can become self-referential: "what the founder wants to say" instead of "what the audience already wants to watch, ask, or compare."

### Offer

> Turn founder expertise into audience-backed video topics and content angles.

### What To Show

- Repeated audience questions.
- Competitor expert channels.
- Watchable topic angles.
- Hook/title examples.
- Short-form cut ideas.
- YouTube/LinkedIn/podcast angle map.

### First CTA

> Send me one founder/client niche. I will return a free mini topic map.

### Paid Next Step

One workspace per founder niche; monthly refresh before content planning.

## 3. PR / Influencer Agencies

### Core Pain

They need campaign ideas, cultural angles, creator briefs, and reasons why a brand should say something now.

### Vulnerability

PR and influencer pitches can be too creative-first and not enough audience-signal-first. WANT2VIEW gives evidence for what people already care about.

### Offer

> Audience-demand scan before PR/influencer campaign ideation.

### What To Show

- Trending video topics in category.
- Creator/content formats that repeat.
- Audience pains/desires/objections.
- Campaign angle ideas.
- Creator brief angles.
- Video hooks that can become PR/social hooks.

### First CTA

> Send one client category. I will send 5-7 campaign/content angles backed by public video signals.

### Paid Next Step

Use a full research report before each campaign pitch or launch.
