# WANT2VIEW GTM / Leadgen Strategy

Date: 2026-06-06

## Core Positioning

WANT2VIEW should not be sold as generic YouTube analytics. The strongest framing is:

> A video-category research engine that turns public content signals into production decisions before teams spend money on scripts, shoots, creators, PR angles, or client strategy.

The product is most valuable where research repeats, takes human time, and affects revenue: agency strategy, content production, creator campaigns, personal-brand growth, and campaign planning.

## Primary ICP Ranking

### 1. YouTube Agencies / Content Factories

Why they are attractive:
- They run repeated content planning cycles.
- They already sell strategy, SEO, scripting, thumbnails, channel management, or production.
- WANT2VIEW can become an internal research layer rather than an extra standalone tool.
- They can buy agency tier or multiple workspaces if it saves strategist/researcher time.

Buyer:
- Founder
- Head of strategy
- YouTube strategist
- Production lead
- Client success lead

Pain:
- Manual competitor research.
- Guessing what topics to produce next.
- Weak client briefs.
- Too much time spent finding references, hooks, title patterns, thumbnails, and outlier videos.

Offer:
> We help YouTube/content teams turn a niche or client channel into a research brief: competitors, outlier videos, audience demand, SEO angles, title/hook patterns, thumbnails, and production recommendations.

Best lead magnet:
> Free mini research for one client niche: 10 competitors, 20 video ideas, 5 content patterns, and 3 immediate production angles.

### 2. PR / Influencer / Creator Marketing Agencies

Why they are attractive:
- They need campaign angles and creator/content fit, not only media lists.
- Video platforms are central to influencer campaigns.
- WANT2VIEW can help find what audiences already react to before a PR/influencer campaign is pitched.

Buyer:
- Founder
- Director of influencer marketing
- PR strategy lead
- Social/content lead
- Campaign strategist

Pain:
- Creating fresh campaign angles.
- Proving why a content idea will resonate.
- Matching brand messages to real audience demand.
- Preparing creator briefs that are not generic.

Offer:
> We help PR and influencer teams find real audience-demand signals before a campaign: trending topics, creator/category patterns, objections, questions, hooks, and content angles.

Best lead magnet:
> Free campaign-angle scan for one brand category: what audiences are already watching, what creators repeat, and which PR/content angles have proof.

### 3. Expert / Personal Brand Agencies

Why they are attractive:
- Their clients have expertise but often lack content-market fit.
- They need repeatable topic systems.
- WANT2VIEW can translate expertise into watchable demand.

Buyer:
- Agency founder
- Personal-brand strategist
- Ghostwriter / content strategist
- YouTube producer

Pain:
- Expert has knowledge, but topics are boring or too internal.
- Content calendar is opinion-based.
- Need angles that sell trust and authority.

Offer:
> We turn an expert's niche into audience-backed video topics, hooks, competitor references, and a simple content roadmap.

### 4. EdTech / Online Schools / Course Businesses

Why they are attractive:
- They need content for acquisition, warm-up, retention, and launches.
- They often have high LTV and can afford research.
- Their topics map well to viewer questions, fears, and objections.

Buyer:
- CMO
- Content lead
- YouTube producer
- Product marketing lead

Pain:
- Need constant educational content ideas.
- Need to know what learners actually ask and fear.
- Need competitive market map before launch.

Offer:
> We show which learning topics, objections, hooks, and formats are already pulling attention in your category.

### 5. B2B Content / PR Agencies

Why they are attractive:
- They increasingly sell thought leadership, founder content, podcasts, webinars, and video.
- WANT2VIEW can map market language and content gaps.

Buyer:
- Founder
- Content strategy lead
- PR strategy lead

Pain:
- Complex topics need clear angles.
- Clients ask for thought leadership but lack audience evidence.
- Video/podcast topics are decided internally.

Offer:
> We help B2B teams find market-backed content angles before writing thought leadership or filming experts.

## Qualification Criteria

Use these filters before adding a lead to outreach:
- Public website is live.
- Contact exists: email, contact form, calendar, or clear business inquiry flow.
- Website mentions at least one of: YouTube, creator, influencer, video production, content strategy, PR, social video, campaign strategy, channel management, SEO, thumbnails, scripting, audience growth.
- They sell services to clients, brands, creators, experts, or businesses.
- They appear to have repeated research/strategy work.

Score leads from 1 to 5:
- 5: explicit YouTube strategy/content production + public email + clear client work.
- 4: influencer/PR/content strategy + public email/contact + strong campaign fit.
- 3: general video/content agency with strategy language.
- 2: weak fit, mainly production without strategy.
- 1: directory, dormant, no clear contact, or questionable business.

## First 14-Day Execution Plan

### Day 1-2: Build List

Target:
- 50 English-language leads.
- Split: 20 YouTube/content factories, 15 PR/influencer agencies, 10 video/content agencies, 5 personal-brand/edtech agencies.

Output:
- CSV with segment, website, contact, reason, personalized hook.

### Day 3-4: Build Sample Assets

Create 3 mini research examples:
- One for a YouTube agency niche.
- One for a PR/influencer brand category.
- One for expert/personal brand niche.

Each sample should be short:
- 5 competitor/channel references.
- 5 outlier/trending videos.
- 5 audience demand signals.
- 5 title/hook patterns.
- 3 production/campaign angles.

### Day 5-8: Send First 50

Use short personalized emails. No long deck. The goal is reply, not sale.

Primary CTA:
> Want me to run a free mini scan for one client niche?

### Day 9-10: Follow Up

Follow-up should include a concrete example:
> For example, if your client is in wellness/finance/SaaS, we can map competitors, outlier topics, viewer questions, and title/thumbnail patterns before the team writes scripts.

### Day 11-14: Convert Replies

If they reply:
- Ask for one niche/client/channel.
- Run a mini scan.
- Offer paid full research or agency workspace.

## Key Strategic Recommendation

Start with agencies, not individual creators.

Individual creators often have lower urgency and lower willingness to pay for research. Agencies and content factories already sell strategy and production. If WANT2VIEW makes them faster, sharper, or more convincing to clients, it becomes a margin tool.

The strongest first wedge:

> "We save your strategy team hours before every client content plan by turning a niche into a ready production research brief."
