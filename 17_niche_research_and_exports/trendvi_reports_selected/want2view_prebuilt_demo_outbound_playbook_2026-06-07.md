# WANT2VIEW Pre-Built Demo Outbound Playbook

Date: 2026-06-07

## Core Decision

Yes: for the best leads, we should prepare research before they ask.

But not for everyone.

Pre-built demos increase conversion because the lead sees real thinking, not a generic SaaS pitch. The tradeoff is time. If we prebuild for 100 leads, we will spend all week polishing demos and send too little outbound. So we use tiers.

## Three Demo Tiers

### Tier 1: Prebuild Before Outreach

Use for:
- priority A leads;
- strong YouTube/content agencies;
- strong personal brand/founder content agencies;
- strong PR/influencer boutiques with a clear client category.

Asset:
- 1-page mini brief;
- 3 strongest patterns;
- 3 suggested angles;
- optional screenshot or demo workspace after reply.

First message angle:
> I looked at your agency and prepared a small sample scan around a likely client niche. Want me to send it?

Or, if we include the short version:
> I ran a small sample scan around `{niche}` and found 3 patterns that may be useful for your client briefs.

### Tier 2: Semi-Prebuild

Use for:
- priority B leads;
- good fit but not enough evidence to spend 60+ minutes.

Asset:
- 3 bullets;
- suggested niche;
- ask if they want the full mini scan.

First message angle:
> Based on your site, I would probably scan `{niche}` first. If useful, I can run the mini research and send it over.

### Tier 3: Ask Niche First

Use for:
- weaker leads;
- form-only contacts;
- broad video agencies;
- unclear niche.

Asset:
- no research before reply.

First message angle:
> Send one niche/client/channel and I will run a free mini scan.

## Demo Account Strategy

Do not send login credentials in cold emails.

Better order:
1. Send useful brief or teaser.
2. Get reply.
3. Prepare/share demo workspace.
4. If needed, create a temporary demo account or invite them.

Why:
- cold credentials look weird;
- people do not explore dashboards without context;
- credentials create operational risk;
- a brief gives them the story first.

Best demo account language:

> I can open a prepared workspace for this niche so your team does not need to search for channels manually. You will see the competitor set, videos, patterns, and brief structure already loaded.

## What A Prebuilt Demo Should Contain

Keep it compact. The lead should understand value in 60 seconds.

Sections:
1. Why this niche was selected.
2. Competitor/reference channels.
3. Fast-moving/outlier video examples.
4. Audience demand signals.
5. Title/hook/format patterns.
6. 3 content or campaign angles.
7. How their agency could use this in client work.

Do not overdo:
- no 20-page report;
- no giant spreadsheet;
- no complex dashboard explanation;
- no "we analyzed everything" claim unless true.

## Execution Rule

Daily rhythm:
- prepare 2 prebuilt demos;
- send 5-10 cold emails;
- update tracker;
- do not let research work block outreach.

The machine only works if both sides move: demos and sends.
